Claudio Di Sipio

About

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Department of Information Engineering Computer Science and Mathematics of the University of L'Aquila. I'm interested in recommendation systems for software engineering, mining OSS repositories, and application of ML techniques for Software Engineering. Recentely, I'm to investigate the low-code paradigm and platforms.

Current role: Ph.D. candidate at University of L'Aquila

  • Birthday: 10 January 1994
  • City: University of L'Aquila
  • Degree: Master degree in Computer Science
